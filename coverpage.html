Mission Statement

Sports Injury Tasmania (SIT) provides advice and advocacy services together with a large range of medical supplies in support of people throughout Tasmania who are suffering from sport induced injuries and to promote the benefits of sport to individuals who spend more time on the sidelines than on the field of play.

Vision Statement

To provide an ongoing support framework utilising the very latest in technology and medical innovation to ensure injury prone sporting professionals can continue to live a productive and healthy life.
